About SmartHat

SmartHat is a wearable designed to improve safety in hazardous work environments. It integrates multiple subsystems to monitor impacts, CO levels, vitals, SMS alerts, and UV exposure.

Subsystems

Impact Detection

Detects impacts to the wearer's head and alerts the user if unsafe forces are detected.

CO Monitoring

Monitors carbon monoxide levels in the environment and notifies the user of hazardous concentrations.

Vitals Monitoring

Tracks vital signs such as heart rate and body temperature to ensure the user's well-being.

SMS Communication

Sends alerts via SMS to supervisors or team members in case of emergencies.

UV Monitoring

Measures UV exposure and alerts the wearer when UV levels become unsafe.
