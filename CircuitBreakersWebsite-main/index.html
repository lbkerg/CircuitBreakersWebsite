About SmartHat

SmartHat is an advanced safety hard hat that continuously monitors both the worker and their environment, detecting hazards in real time and instantly communicating alerts. By integrating impact sensing, vital sign monitoring, gas detection, UV and ambient light measurement, and wireless SMS notifications into a single device, it transforms a standard piece of equipment into a proactive safety system.

In high-risk industries like construction, mining, and manufacturing (where dangers such as falling objects, toxic gas buildup, poor visibility, and medical emergencies are ever-present) SmartHat provides critical awareness that traditional hard hats lack. This all-in-one solution helps prevent accidents before they escalate, keeping workers safer and giving supervisors the information they need to respond immediately.

Features

Impact Detection

Detects impacts to the wearer's head and alerts the user if unsafe forces are detected.

CO Monitoring

Monitors carbon monoxide levels in the environment and notifies the user of hazardous concentrations.

Vitals Monitoring

Monitors heart rate to detect signs of fatigue, stress, or danger.

Cellular Communication

Sends alerts to supervisor's or team member's smart device in case of emergencies.

UV Monitoring & Headlamp

Monitors for unsafe UV levels and automatically controls the headlamp based on ambient light.
